FAQ

      
Clear limits = fewer disputes. This is what ACKZERO does ‚Äî and what it does not.


      

        

          

            Does ACKZERO store my message?
            
No. This page hashes locally. ACKZERO stores hashes + timestamps + proof events only.

          


          

            Does this prove the recipient read the message?
            
No. It proves a receipt was created and can record that the claim link (or pixel) was opened. Reading the full content is not guaranteed.

          


          

            Is this equivalent to registered electronic delivery?
            
No. ACKZERO is a technical receipt. Legal equivalence depends on jurisdiction and regulated providers.

          


          

            What should I include in my email?
            
Include the claim link. Optionally include the receipt link for transparency and faster verification.

          


          

            What if I need stronger evidence?
            
Use ACKZERO + keep a copy of the sent message (PDF/screenshot) + include the receipt JSON in your evidence file. For regulated equivalence, use a qualified e-delivery provider in your jurisdiction.
