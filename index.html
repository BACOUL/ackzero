FAQ

      
Short answers, no ambiguity.


      

        

          

            Does ACKZERO store my message?
            
No. Hashes are computed locally. ACKZERO stores hashes + timestamps + proof event metadata only.

          

          

            Does this prove the recipient read my message?
            
No. It proves issuance and availability of the claim/receipt. Reading is not guaranteed.

          

          

            Is this equivalent to registered electronic delivery?
            
No. ACKZERO is a technical receipt. Legal equivalence depends on jurisdiction and regulated providers.

          

          

            What should I keep for my case file?
            
Keep the receipt link (and JSON). Also keep your sent email copy if relevant.
