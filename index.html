          FAQ
          Clear limits
        

        

          

            Does ACKZERO store my message?
            
No. This page hashes locally. ACKZERO stores hashes and timestamps only.

          

          

            Does this prove the recipient read the message?
            
No. It proves a receipt was created and can record that the claim link was opened (availability). Reading is not guaranteed.

          

          

            Is this equivalent to registered electronic delivery?
            
No. ACKZERO is a technical receipt. Legal equivalence depends on jurisdiction and regulated providers.

          

          

            What should I include in my email?
            
Include the claim link. Optionally include the receipt link for transparency.

          

          

            What exactly is signed?
            
The receipt JSON is signed using HMAC-SHA256 so it can be checked for tampering against the service’s verification rules.
