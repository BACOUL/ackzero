FAQ Clear limits
Does ACKZERO store my message?

No. Your browser hashes locally. ACKZERO stores hashes + timestamps only.

Does this prove delivery or that the recipient read it?

No. It proves a receipt was issued at a precise time for a specific message (hash-only). Delivery and reading depend on your email provider and the recipient.

Can the recipient verify the proof?

Yes. The receipt JSON link can be shared and checked by anyone.

Is this equivalent to registered electronic delivery?

No. ACKZERO is a technical receipt. For regulated equivalence, use an accredited provider in your jurisdiction.

Legal positioning (v0.1) Important

ACKZERO provides a technical proof of issuance and a verifiable receipt. It does not certify legal identity, does not guarantee delivery, and is not a regulated e-delivery service.
