        About “verification”
        

          Anyone can fetch the receipt JSON and inspect its fields and timestamps. Cryptographic validation depends on the receipt’s signature scheme and canonicalization rules.
          In v0.1, this page focuses on: (1) fetching the public receipt, and (2) letting you locally match your own message hashes against it.

FAQ

      
Short answers, no ambiguity.


      

        

          

            Does ACKZERO store my message?
            
No. This page hashes locally. ACKZERO stores hashes and timestamps only.

          

          

            Does this prove the recipient read the message?
            
No. It can record that the claim link was opened (availability). Reading is not guaranteed.

          

          

            Is this equivalent to registered electronic delivery?
            
No. ACKZERO is a technical receipt. Legal equivalence depends on jurisdiction and regulated providers.

          

          

            What should I include in my email?
            
Include the claim link. Optionally include the receipt link for transparency.

          

          

            What should I keep for my case file?
            
Keep the receipt link and the receipt JSON (download/copy). If relevant, keep your sent email copy too.
